‌Switch Appearance‌

‌ uses Arc as it's default theme package. It uses Arc theme for Gtk windows, panels, login window and lock screen and Moka as icon theme.‌

‌Another emerging design trend is Material Design which is basically a collection of design guidelines introduced by Google to bring a uniform appearance across different platforms. There are different theme packages inspired by Google's Material Design style.‌

‌As values the freedom of users, we provide easy option to switch between our default Arc style and Material style.‌
